About Me

Hi there, I'm Clinton Otieno
A passionate Full-Stack Software Engineer with a love for solving complex problems through elegant code and creative solutions. My tech stack spans across JavaScript (React, Node.js, Express) and Python (Django), along with experience in MongoDB and PostgreSQL. I thrive in both front-end and back-end development, always looking to bridge the gap between functional design and powerful engineering. With a background in cyber security and online safety, I bring a security-first mindset to all the projects I work on, ensuring robust, scalable, and secure applications. Whether it’s crafting dynamic web applications or building out efficient, secure APIs, I’m all about delivering high-quality work that pushes boundaries. When I'm not coding, you can usually find me exploring the latest trends in infosec or dabbling in new technologies that enhance user experiences. I’m constantly learning and believe in the power of collaboration, so feel free to reach out if you're interested in connecting or working together!
